Main Rewrites

The important rewrites and gameplay changes are that every toon starts with 4 task slots. Levels are new and influence witch gags you can have and prestige. Laff is based on both your main levels and activity levels. Kudos tasks are tasks for specific places after all of the main-line tasks have been acomplished. Kudos give many buffs to the areas that you complete them in. The story has been largely rewritten with the toon you play eventually fighting against the upper echelons of the cogs HQ.
